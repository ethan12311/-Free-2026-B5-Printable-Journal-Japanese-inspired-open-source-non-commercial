## [Free] 2026 B5 Printable Journal — Japanese-inspired, open-source, non-commercial**
>
> Hi everyone! I made a 92-page B5 printable yearly journal for 2026, inspired by Hobonichi Techo and Takahashi Shoten. It's completely free, open-source, and non-commercial (CC BY-NC 4.0).
>
> **Features:**
> - Japanese frontispiece with 一期一會 (Ichigo Ichie)
> - 12 monthly calendar pages (Monday-first, CJK+English weekday labels)
> - 4-5 weekly writing pages per month (each week = one full B5 page)
> - 15 guided reflection prompts (monthly summaries + year-end batch)
> - Strictly Cormorant Garamond serif — zero "AI taste"
> - B5 (176×250mm), print-ready
>
> **Tech note:** The journal was completely prompted and powered by GLM 5.2 (Z.ai's AI), with human art direction. All design decisions (font choice, layout, prompt wording) were human-driven.
>
